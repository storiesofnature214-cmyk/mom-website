Dear Mom,

Sometimes I still just can't believe that you are gone. I know you can't come back, but I still keep hoping, even if it's only in my dreams. You were the most beautiful part of my life, Mom, and nothing in this world will ever replace that. I carry your strength and kindness with me every day. The hardest part is remembering you and missing you in silence. You taught me everything except how to live without you. I would choose you to be my mom in every lifetime, again and again. I love you, Mom. Today, tomorrow, and always.
